## The Problem

The client was managing inventory with Excel spreadsheets across three departments. Data was duplicated, errors were frequent, and reporting took days.

## What I Built

A full Windows desktop ERP application with:

- **Inventory management** — real-time stock tracking across warehouses
- **Order processing** — end-to-end order lifecycle management
- **Reporting dashboard** — automated daily/weekly reports
- **Role-based access** — different views for managers, staff, and admins

## What I Learned

This project taught me that software for non-technical users needs to feel invisible. Every feature I built was tested with the actual warehouse staff — not just the manager who commissioned it.

The biggest challenge wasn't the code. It was understanding the workflow deeply enough to simplify it.

## Result

Reduced reporting time from 2 days to 20 minutes. Zero data duplication incidents in the first month after launch.
